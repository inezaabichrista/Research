Safety Guidelines

  1. Always remain seated while the vehicle is moving and use seatbelts where available.
  2. Keep personal belongings secured and within sight.
  3. Report suspicious behavior via the Report a Problem page or inform the driver.
  4. Use official stops and avoid isolated waiting areas at night.
  5. Follow traffic officer instructions and posted signage for safe boarding and alighting.
