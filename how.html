How it works

  1. Choose your role (Passenger) on the home page and click Start.
  2. Enter contact details on the Dashboard and click Next.
  3. Fill passenger details and select route/date in the Booking page.
  4. Confirm booking — it will be saved locally (simulated payment).
  5. Use the Contact or Report pages for support or issues.
